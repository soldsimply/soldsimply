Your practitioner is originally from Copenhagen, Denmark.

She is a graduate of The Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College and the Central Maryland School of Massage.

Upon graduating from Chiropractic School, she practiced as a Chiropractor in the DC area for five years. She then became a full time mom, and now that her children are older she has a Massage Therapy practice in North Potomac.

She enjoys working with women and children of all ages. Her massages are customized to meet the specific needs and preferences of each individual person, and while her touch is light and soothing when treating children and sensitive clients, other clients appreciate her proficiency in Deep Tissue Massage and Trigger Point Therapy. She encourages client feedback in order to design a massage that is just right for you.

Many of her clients receive massages on a regular basis, either to manage a chronic condition, to help their muscles recover from working out, or simply for stress relief and relaxation.

She has a special interest in Orthopedic Massage with a focus on neck pain, shoulder pain, back pain, and headaches.
