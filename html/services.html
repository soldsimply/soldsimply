Services

Swedish Massage consists of long flowing massage strokes and some kneading of the muscles. The depth of pressure is light to medium. This technique promotes a general feeling of relaxation and an increase in circulation.

Sports Massage is a branch of Swedish Massage in which the practitioner focuses on specific areas. This type of massage is used to prepare the muscles for an event or to aid in the recovery from exercise.

Orthopedic Massage is used in the treatment of the muscular component of specific conditions. Examples include Tension Headaches, Neck Pain, Back Pain, Rotator Cuff Tendinitis, Tennis Elbow, Golfer's Elbow, Piriformis Syndrome, IT Band Syndrome, Chondromalacia Patella, and Plantar faciitis.

Deep Tissue Massage targets muscles located below the superficial layer of muscles. The strokes are slow and deep. This technique is especially helpful for chronically tense areas.

in Trigger Point Therapy static pressure is applied to tender spots in the muscles. This technique is very useful in the treatment of chronic pain. It is common to experience some soreness lasting for 1-2 days after the treatment.

All massages are customized to meet the specific needs and preferences of each individual client, and a combination of the above techniques is used in the design of the massage.
